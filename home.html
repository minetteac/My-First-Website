Hello! I'm Minette Carascal, a Mechanical Engineer with a passion for innovation and a drive to expand my skills into the world of programming. I graduated with a Mechanical Engineering degree from University of Negros Occidental Recoletos and successfully passed the Mechanical Engineering Licensure Examination on August of 2023. My academic journey helped me gain skills in design, problem-solving, and project management. Despite lacking a background in programming, I'm excited about what lies ahead. I recognize that mastering it will require dedication and time, but I'm prepared to begin with the fundamentals and progress from there. I'm eager about the opportunities programming presents and anticipate the learning and development journey ahead. I dream of one day combining it with my mechanical engineering knowledge.
